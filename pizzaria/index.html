About us

Ai Ai Favela is a Brazilian restaurant where the objetive is to deliver the most delicious Brazilian pizza in Australia. We have a variety of pizzas, both savory and sweet.
We have a qualified team to provide expert service from the customer until the pizza is delivered to your home.

Brazilian pizzas

At the Brazilian pizzaria Ai Ai Favela you will find the largest variety of Brazilian pizzas, with an unmistakable flavor and quality ingredients, making it the best pizza in the region.
